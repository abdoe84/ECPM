CEO Meeting with Environment & Sustainability Sector Team

The CEO, Eng. Nasser Al-Mutairi, met with the Environment & Sustainability Sector team on 8 September 2025.

During the meeting, the Sector Director presented a brief overview of the sector’s achievements, along with the accomplishments of each division. Each team member also introduced themselves and their work.

The CEO emphasized providing full support to help the sector grow, in line with the company’s policy of development and expansion.
